International Trauma Life Support

Duration
2 Days
Class Size
Up to 8 participants
CPD Credits
23 CEUs (including 1 CEU for Ethics)

Target Audience:

Any registered medical professional — doctors, nurses, and EMS staff — who may be expected to assess and manage patients experiencing an acute traumatic event. Both basic and advanced versions are offered.

Topics Covered:

  • Scene safety and triage
  • Trauma victim assessment and initial care
  • Airway management in trauma
  • Shock and haemorrhage control
  • Head and spinal trauma
  • Chest, abdominal, and extremity trauma
  • Burns management
  • Trauma in pregnant patients, children, and elderly populations
  • Trauma cardiac arrest

Practical Skill Stations:

  • Haemorrhage control including tourniquets
  • IV and IO access
  • Tension pneumothorax decompression
  • Spinal motion restriction and patient extrication
  • Extremity stabilisation

Assessment and Preparation:

A minimum score of 74% on the written assessment and successful completion of a simulated scenario are required for certification.

Certificate: ITLS International Certificate
