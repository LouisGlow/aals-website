“The aim of the Academy of Advanced Life Support is to help registered Doctors, Nurses and Advanced Life Support Paramedics, regardless of their level of skill and knowledge, improve their understanding, ability and confidence with respect to the techniques of resuscitation and emergency care.”

“

MB,BCh(Wits), DipPEC(SA), FCEM(SA), FERC(Belg), FAHA (USA) — Registered Specialist in Emergency Medicine. Hon. Adjunct Professor, Division of Emergency Medicine, University of the Witwatersrand. Medical Director, Academy of Advanced Life Support. Hon. Life President, Resuscitation Council of Southern Africa. Past President, College of Emergency Medicine of South Africa.

Professor Walter Kloeck’s post-graduate medical career started in 1978 with an intense interest in Resuscitation and Emergency Medicine, and he has devoted his life to raising the standard of emergency care both in South Africa and world-wide. Walter co-founded the Resuscitation Council of Southern Africa in 1988 and was Chairman for over 20 years, co-founder of the International Liaison Committee on Resuscitation (ILCOR), which represents all major Resuscitation Councils worldwide, co-founder of the Emergency Medicine Society of South Africa, and co-founder and Inaugural President of the College of Emergency Medicine of South Africa.

Having co-authored 80 international peer-reviewed publications and over 3,750 citations relating to resuscitation and emergency cardiovascular care, Walter was awarded the International Fellowship of the American Heart Association (FAHA) in recognition of his contribution to resuscitation both nationally and internationally.

”
Professor Walter G J Kloeck

Courses We Offer

Basic Life Support (BLS)

The Course is designed for any healthcare worker who has a voluntary or professional duty to respond to a cardiac or respiratory emergency.

Click to Learn More →

Advanced Cardiovascular Life Support Provider (ACLS)

The Course is available to any healthcare professional registered as a Medical or Dental Practitioner, Intern, Junior Intern, Professional Nurse or Advanced Life Support Paramedic. Participants must hold a valid AHA BLS for Healthcare Provider Certificate.

Click to Learn More →

ACLS for Experienced Providers (ACLS-EP)

The Course is designed for healthcare professionals in possession of a current AHA ACLS Provider Certificate. Ideal for EMS, Emergency Department and ICU staff, and medical practitioners who regularly participate in resuscitation.

Click to Learn More →

Paediatric Advanced Life Support Provider (PALS)

The Course is available to any registered healthcare professional. Participants must hold a valid AHA BLS Provider Certificate. Prior ACLS participation is recommended.

Click to Learn More →

Paediatric ALS for Experienced Providers (PALS-EP)

The Course is available to any healthcare professional registered as a Medical or Dental Practitioner, Professional Nurse or ALS Paramedic with a valid PALS Provider Certificate.

Click to Learn More →

Advanced Medical Life Support for Doctors (AMLS)

The Advanced Medical Life Support Course is particularly designed for medical practitioners who may be called upon to recognise and manage a patient with an acute or life-threatening medical emergency.

Click to Learn More →

Emergency ECG and Pharmacology

The Course is designed for all healthcare professionals whose responsibilities require them to recognise and interpret life-threatening cardiac rhythms and provide initial emergency care and pharmacological interventions.

Click to Learn More →

Advanced Neonatal Life Support (NRP)

The Course is the evidence-based Neonatal Resuscitation Program designed by the American Academy of Pediatrics in association with the American Heart Association for healthcare professionals whose responsibilities may require them to manage or participate in the resuscitation of a newborn.

Click to Learn More →

ACLS and PALS Update Course

A renewal course for healthcare professionals with an AHA ACLS or PALS Provider Certificate that is about to expire. Includes mandatory online pre-course preparation.

Click to Learn More →

International Trauma Life Support (ITLS)

ITLS teaches a systematic approach to the assessment and management of patients experiencing an acute traumatic event using latest evidence-based guidelines.
