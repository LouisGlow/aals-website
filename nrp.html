Advanced Neonatal Life Support (NRP)

Duration
1 Day
Class Size
Up to 12 participants
CPD Credits
18 CEUs (including 1 CEU for Ethics)

Target Audience:

Healthcare professionals whose responsibilities may require them to manage or participate in the resuscitation of a newborn. A valid AHA BLS for Healthcare Provider Certificate is required.

Learning Outcomes:

Based on the evidence-based Neonatal Resuscitation Program designed by the American Academy of Pediatrics in association with the American Heart Association, participants will be able to:

  • Perform initial neonatal resuscitation steps
  • Use ventilation devices correctly
  • Apply chest compression techniques
  • Perform endotracheal intubation
  • Administer medications
  • Manage special birth considerations
  • Care for preterm infants
  • Apply ethical principles and end-of-life care considerations

Course Structure:

Practical, case-based program with video demonstrations, instructor guidance, and hands-on practice with simulated clinical scenarios.

Assessment and Preparation:

A 270-page American Academy of Pediatrics Textbook of Neonatal Resuscitation plus a Multimedia DVD-ROM (including resuscitation footage, laryngoscopic imagery, interactive scenarios, and 3-D animations) are supplied before the Course.

Certificate: Resuscitation Council of Southern Africa and South African Paediatric Association Advanced Neonatal Life Support Certificate
